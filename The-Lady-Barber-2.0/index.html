About

Hi! I'm Deonna. I am a second-generation lady barber. My mom, Jewel Fortner, was the first lady barber in Florence. In 1999, I started my career at Roger's Barber Stylists, where I worked for 21 years. In 2021, I branched off on my own to open The Lady Barber.
